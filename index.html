About Us

100% Handmade Lucknowi Craft

Welcome to House of Chikankari

Established in Delhi, House of Chikankari represents the pinnacle of traditional Indian ethnic wear. We bring you the authentic charm of Lucknow's historic Chikankari work, meticulously handcrafted by skilled artisans who have preserved this delicate shadow-work embroidery for generations.

Our store specializes in premium ladies' fabrics and tailored pieces, catering to modern tastes while honoring timeless designs. Whether you are looking for ready-to-wear stitched suits for a special event or premium unstitched suit sets that you can tailor to your own body shape, we have you covered.

How to Measure Properly

1
Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal.
2
Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, typically the narrowest point.
3
Hips: Stand with feet together and measure around the widest part of your hips.
4
Shoulders: Measure from the edge of one shoulder bone across your back to the other.
5
Full Length: Measure from the high point of your shoulder down to the desired hemline.
